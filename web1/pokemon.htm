Pokémon Go!

The new generation of the videogame series, Pokémon has been a popular topic since its release in July, 2016. Pokémon Go which is based on the concept of augmented reality, allows users to play in a real-time environment. This videogame app is designed for iOS and Android operating systems as well. Pokémon Go can be downloaded as free-to-play app that lets users capture and train Pokémon.

Interesting Facts abouth Pokémon Go

  • Series: Pokémon, Generation VI
  • Developer & Publisher: Niantic
  • Artist: Dennis Hwang
  • Composer: Junichi Masuda
  • Technology: It uses GPS and camera of smartphone to track Pokémon.
  • More than 75 million downloads shortly after the game's release

3 Steps to go Pokémon

  1. Get your trainer ready by choosing a style and accessories for him.
  2. Catch your first Pokémon and add to your pokedex. Increase your levels by adding more powerful Pokémon to your Pokédex.
  3. Join a team when you are ready for it and go for a battle.
